Data deletion

Effective date: May 12, 2026 · Pawffles · Morsa Software

Summary: You can delete all of your Pawffles data from inside the app at any time. If you don't have access to the app, email us at morsasoftware@gmail.com and we'll do it for you.

1. How to delete your data from inside the app

  1. Open Pawffles.
  2. Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner of the level select screen.
  3. Tap "Delete my data".
  4. Confirm in the dialog that appears.

Deletion is immediate. The app automatically starts a fresh anonymous session so you can keep playing if you want.

2. How to delete your data without app access

If you uninstalled the app, lost your device, or otherwise can't use the in-app flow, email us at morsasoftware@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my data — Pawffles".

We need your nickname exactly as it appears in the weekly leaderboard so we can find your row. We process manual requests within 30 days of receiving the email.

3. What gets deleted

  • Your local progress (unlocked levels, best scores, stars, daily best scores).
  • Your nickname stored locally and in our database.
  • Your rows in the weekly leaderboard (weekly_scores table).
  • Your nickname reservation (user_nicknames table).
  • Your anonymous authentication session.

4. What is retained

We retain an anonymous authentication row with no personal information (no email, no phone, no real name). That row holds only a random internal identifier with no way to link it back to you as a person, and it exists only for technical reasons of the authentication provider (Supabase).

Anonymous usage events in PostHog (which screens you tapped) are tied to a separate identifier and are retained per PostHog's retention policy (12 months by default on the free tier).

5. Scope

This policy applies to Pawffles (Android package: com.morsasoftware.pawffles), published by Morsa Software.

6. Contact

Questions or manual requests: morsasoftware@gmail.com.
